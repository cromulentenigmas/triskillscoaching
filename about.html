Who We Are

A professional strength and endurance coach since 2004, Coach Anthony is certified through USA Triathlon and holds a personal training certification from the American Council on Exercise (ACE). He's worked with clients of all ages and abilities both in person (within the tri-state area) and remotely online both domestically and internationally. He has coached athletes in all multisport and running distances including the Marathon and Ironman and has had athletes qualify for the Boston Marathon, 70.3 Worlds, USAT Nationals and Norseman Xtreme Triathlon.

He is a 2X Ironman finisher, 7x Marathon finisher and multiple Age Group winner. He has raced all over the country pursuing his passion for multi sport endurance racing.
